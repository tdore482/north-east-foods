Who We Are

About North East Foods

North East Foods Limited is a private company limited by shares incorporated under the Companies Act No 15 of 2013 in Malawi. Its main office is located in Blantyre, Malawi.

The Directors of the company bring with them a combined industry experience of over 30 years of business and financial management experience. The company offers a strong financial backbone ensuring swift operations and sustainability of business undertakings.

North East Foods brings on board valuable experience in the production, purchasing, logistics and export of different varieties of whole dried chilies, Black Tea, Kidney beans, groundnuts, pigeon peas and soybeans predominantly to countries that are involved in large scale processing of such agricultural produce.

To date, the enterprise has exported their products to Spain, Italy, France, South Africa, Germany, United Kingdom and Netherlands on a contractual basis since 2014. North East Foods is a registered member of the Malawi Investment and Trade Center (MITC) and The Tea and Coffee Merchants Association of Malawi.

Our Mission
